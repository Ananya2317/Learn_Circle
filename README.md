# Learn_Circle
LearnCircle is a lightweight learning platform built around small, skill-focused learning circles. Each circle is created by someone who wants to share knowledgeâ€”whether through notes, links, tutorials, or curated study materials. Students join these circles to access resources, stay organized, and learn together.

# How It Works
1. Anyone can start a learning circle by uploading study materials or linking helpful resources. The more useful and active a circle becomes, the more recognition its creator earns.

2. Students follow circles that match their interests or skill goals. Inside each circle, members can:
- Access shared notes and content
- Complete tasks or mini-assignments
- Chat with others learning the same topic
- Earn Recognition and Rewards

3. When a circle grows or its materials get used consistently, the creator is rewarded. This can include:
- Points that track contribution
- Badges and achievement levels
- A reputation score within the platform
- Potential monetisation opportunities in the future
This reward system encourages creators to continually improve their content, while learners benefit from higher-quality resources.

# Why LearnCircle?

Community-first learning: Small groups make collaboration natural and less overwhelming.

Encouraged contribution: Creators stay motivated because their work is recognised.

Skill-based growth: Every circle focuses on a specific topic or ability, helping learners stay on track.

Future-ready: The reward system aims to grow into a full creator-economy model for educational content.

LearnCircle aims to make study communities more meaningful, resource-driven, and rewarding for everyone involved.
